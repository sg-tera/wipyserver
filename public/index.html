Why Choose Wipy?

Wipy automates the blackboard cleaning process, giving teachers more time to focus on teaching.

👩‍🏫 Teacher-Friendly

Designed to be easy to set up and operate — no tech skills required.

🤖 Smart Automation

Program Wipy to clean on a schedule so your classroom stays tidy effortlessly.
